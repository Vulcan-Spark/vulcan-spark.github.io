I am an aspiring physician-scientist dedicated to bridging the gap between clinical neurology and basic science. My work leverages spatial transcriptomics, high-dimensional data analysis, and computational modeling to decode the cellular and molecular mechanisms of neurodegenerative diseases. I am also deeply committed to advancing neuroscience education and research in Africa through community initiatives.
