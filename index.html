About Me

MbChb Candidate

I am an MBChB Candidate at College of Health Science, Obafemi Awolowo University with interest cognitive neurology and computational neuroscience.

My work spans Omics (Allen Brain Cell Atlas / MERFISH), Neuroimaging (FreeSurfer, FSL, MRIQC), Electrophysiology, and d — united by a commitment to having a multiscale view of brain disease mechanisms and making neuroscience infrastructure stronger across Africa.

As Co-Founder of NeuroAspire Foundation, I have directly empowered over 200 high school students across Nigeria through workshops, bootcamps, and mentorship.

Interactive Tool

Hippocampal Glial Atlas App

A fully interactive web application built to explore the spatial cell-type landscape of the mouse hippocampus, built on the Allen Brain Cell Atlas MERFISH dataset (65,000+ cells).

  • 🗺️
    Cell Type Map — spatial scatter plot of 4,500+ cells colored by cell type or gene expression
  • 🔬
    Region Filter — filter by hippocampal subregions (CA1, CA3, DG and more)
  • 📊
    Gene Expression — visualize expression patterns of specific marker genes across space
  • ⚡
    Real-time — built with Python + Streamlit, fully deployed and publicly accessible
